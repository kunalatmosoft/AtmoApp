Co-Founder & C.E.O
~Kunal Singh

Kunal Singh's journey to founding the tech giant Atmosoft began in a small town with big dreams. Armed with a passion for technology and an unyielding determination, Kunal pursued his studies in computer science, excelling at every step. Upon graduation, he took a leap of faith, leaving a stable job to follow his entrepreneurial instincts. In 2010, Kunal started Atmosoft from his garage, envisioning a company that would revolutionize the tech industry. His relentless work ethic and innovative mindset quickly set Atmosoft apart. The company initially focused on developing software solutions that seamlessly integrated with everyday life, making technology accessible and user-friendly. Kunal's breakthrough came with the development of a groundbreaking AI platform that transformed industries, from healthcare to finance. This innovation propelled Atmosoft into the spotlight, attracting investors and top talent from around the globe. Under Kunal's visionary leadership, Atmosoft expanded its portfolio to include cutting-edge products in cloud computing, cybersecurity, and IoT. Despite rapid growth, Kunal maintained a culture of creativity and collaboration, fostering an environment where ideas thrived. His commitment to social responsibility also led Atmosoft to launch initiatives aimed at bridging the digital divide. Today, Atmosoft stands as a testament to Kunal Singh's unwavering dedication and visionary leadership, a beacon of innovation in the tech world. His journey from a modest beginning to leading a global tech powerhouse inspires countless aspiring entrepreneurs.
